Handmade Visual Poem

Ekphrastic poem of Leslie Thornton's Handmade (2023)

By: Megan Robinson

Txt Img Multi

speaking over
speaking under
speaking through
the water, a chaos [Ilya Prigogine & Isabelle Stengers]
or the idea of chaos
the edge of the idea
which bleeds and frays
knits loose threads into a sentence
unravels sentences to rain
and back again
begin, again
the sign is too full
it must repeat again
here, through
cracked phone screens
each reflection
a shard of something
new rain sheathing
down like glass
cutting repetition into earth
breathing change into a body
violent [duality]
